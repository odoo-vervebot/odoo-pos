Level Up the security by restricting the orders user wise in POS!!

By default, in Odoo, all the orders of Point of Sale are visible to every user. But sometimes we wish that there should be a restriction in Orders, in terms of visibility.

Hence, we have introduced our module Vervebot POS Display Orders User-Wise, using which admin can restrict the user to view the Orders of his POS only. So accordingly the particular user can view only his POS Orders.

Maintain the regimen at your POS Store efficiently

Restricting visibility at POS is the most important feature as of now for maintaining security in POS. It mainly saves your POS store from harmful threats and malwares. Also, it avoids data leakage into wrong hands.

As we know, all the orders of Point of Sale are visible to every user by default. However, due to security purposes, it's important to restrict the visibility of orders in POS too.
